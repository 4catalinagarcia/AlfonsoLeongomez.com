About Alfonso

Two worlds.
One artist.

Alfonso Leongomez is a multidisciplinary artist whose life moves between two passions that, at first glance, might seem opposed — yet share the same beating heart: the body in motion, pushed to its limits, turned into art.

As a circus and street performer, he transforms public spaces into stages — juggling, acrobatics, physical comedy, and aerial work that stop crowds in their tracks. As a certified Pilates and movement coach, he helps students reconnect with their bodies, build strength from the inside out, and find their own flow.

Movement & Wellness

Fitness & Classes

Rooted in circus training principles, Alfonso's fitness approach builds real-world strength, mobility, and body awareness.

🧘

Pilates

Mat and reformer Pilates sessions grounded in classical method and enriched by movement arts. All levels welcome.

💪

Circus Conditioning

Strength and flexibility training inspired by circus arts. Build the functional body of a performer — without ever stepping in a ring.

🌀

Movement Coaching

One-on-one or small group sessions focused on posture, coordination, mobility, and body intelligence. Ideal for performers, athletes, and anyone seeking to move better.

🎓

Workshops

Intensive workshops combining circus skills and fitness fundamentals. Great for corporate team-building, schools, and community events.
